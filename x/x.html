Kerela Scholarship Scheme

State Merit Scholarship (SMS)

Students who have secured A+ grade in all subjects in the SSLC (10th standard) examination.

Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship (SJMS)

Students who have achieved A+ grades in all subjects in the SSLC examination and are pursuing higher secondary education

District Merit Scholarship (DMS)

Students who have secured A+ grades in all subjects in the SSLC examination in their respective districts

Sanskrit Scholarship(SS)

Students who have achieved A+ grades in Sanskrit in the SSLC examination.

Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students

Students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) studying in classes 1 to 10.

Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students

Financial assistance for those studying in classes 11 to postgraduate levels.

Minority Scholarship (Pre-Matric and Post-Matric)

Students from minority communities (Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Parsis, and Jains) studying in classes 1 to postgraduate levels.

Central Sector Scholarship Scheme

Students who have performed well in their Class 12 examinations.
